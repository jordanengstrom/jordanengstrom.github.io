About

Jordan Engstrom

Pythonista

Currently working as a technical data analyst at Clearwater Analytics in Boise, Idaho. I use Python to build out automated data checks for clients, account managers, and reporting purposes. I also use Python/tkinter when building small GUI tools for account managers' ad hoc tasks. Python is my passion! I graduated Boise State University in fall 2017 with degrees in applied mathematics and geophysics, and attended Boise CodeWorks full stack software development course right afterwards. I have had a lot of experience designing and implementing computational algorithms. My true passion is not only creating those algorithms, but also creating something beautiful, artistic, and usable from the results. I am motivated to work at the intersection of creativity and logic, and be part of a team creating inspiring software that has never existed.
